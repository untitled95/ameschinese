Our Mission

爱城华文学院是在美国注册的非营利组织，其服务对象为Story County所有的K-12学生。

学院以”走进来，带出去“为宗旨。走进来：将学生招进来，学习中文以及传统文化，增强民族自信。带出去：让学生将传统文化传播到社区，并以此服务社区。

学院使用暨南大学出版的《中文》授课，语言文化并重，寓教于乐。以传统文化为核心内容， 学院秋季组织中秋节和传统文化节两大节目。春季组织农历新年庆祝和结业庆典等大型活动。

学院与当地相关机构团体建立了良好的联系与长期稳定的合作。拥有这些丰富的资源，学院不仅能够为学生提供多元服务，自身也成为当地华人社区一个重要的的交流互动平台。

爱城华文学院学生，家长及师职齐心协力，为传承文化，传播中文而携手共进。

Ames Chinese Language Academy (ACLA) is a 501(c)(3) non-for-profit, non-religious, non-political organization serving K-12 students of Story County, Iowa.

ACLA’s mission is “Coming in, Going out.” Coming in - the students come into the school to learn the Chinese language and culture. Going out - the students serve as ambassadors to foster the appreciation of the Chinese cultural heritage and cultural diversity in the community.

The curriculum of ACLA is based on traditional Chinese culture and we use the textbook “Chinese” published by Ji Nan University. The school has two major events in the Fall semester: Mid-Autumn Festival (September) and Traditional Culture Festival (October). In the Spring, the school hosts Chinese New Year Festival (January/February) and ACLA Year End Fair (May).

Over the years, ACLA has built strong partnerships with several local organizations to provide services and resources to our students. The school has also become a good platform for the Chinese community to showcase its culture, talent, and ethnic confidence.

The students, families and staff of ACLA are dedicated to work together to enrich our community with the Chinese language and culture heritage, we welcome you to join us!
